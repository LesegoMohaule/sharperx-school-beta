Starting with Grade 12 Mathematics. Expanding to Grades 8-12 Maths, Physical Sciences and Accounting.

Simple promise

Explain it, help me try it, then mark me.

SharperX is being built as a practical study companion for learners, parents and teachers. It supports homework, revision and exam practice while keeping teachers in control of the real learning programme.

For learners

Study help that meets the learner where they are

Learners can take a photo of a question, speak a voice note or type their working. The app responds with short, step-by-step guidance and then asks the learner to try.

01

Photo homework help

Upload a question photo and get a clear explanation of the concept and first steps.

02

Voice questions

Ask long questions by voice note instead of typing, useful when learners are stuck.

03

Practice mode

Generate CAPS-style practice questions by grade, subject and topic.

04

Mark my answer

Learners enter their steps and final answer, then receive corrections and weak-area feedback.

Subjects

Beta pilot

A controlled school pilot before public launch

The pilot is designed to be small, measurable and safe. The school can select a limited learner group, collect feedback and decide whether the product is useful before any wider rollout.

Safety and privacy

Built as a support tool, not a replacement for teachers

Teacher-led use

SharperX supports practice and revision. It does not replace the teacher, textbook, school assessment or DBE memo.

POPIA-aware controls

Pilot access can be limited, learner details can be minimized, and parent or school consent can be included where required.

Clear disclaimers

AI answers are educational support and must be checked against teacher guidance, official curriculum and official memos.

Wider platform

One interface for learning, electrical guidance and communication

SharperX is also being developed with VoIP calling and an electrical assistant mode. For schools, the beta focus is the tutor mode: photo questions, voice questions, practice, marking and progress feedback.
