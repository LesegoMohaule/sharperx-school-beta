Refund Policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026

1. Digital Service

SharperX sells access to digital app features and usage credits. No physical product is supplied.

2. Refund Requests

Refund requests may be sent to lesegomohaule@gmail.com. Please include the account owner name, email address, payment date, payment reference and reason for the request.

3. When Refunds May Be Approved

SharperX may approve a refund where:

  • a duplicate payment was made by mistake;
  • payment was successful but credits were not allocated and the issue cannot be corrected;
  • the account could not be activated because of a SharperX system fault;
  • a refund is required by applicable South African consumer law.

4. Used Credits

Credits or monthly access that have already been used are generally not refundable, except where required by law or where SharperX confirms that a system fault caused the issue.

5. Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed back through the original payment method where possible. Bank and payment gateway processing times may vary.

6. Cancellations

If recurring billing is introduced, the account owner may request cancellation before the next billing date. Cancellation stops future billing; it does not automatically refund credits already purchased or used.
