Shipping Policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026

1. No Physical Shipping

SharperX is a digital app and online service. No physical goods are sold or shipped. There are no courier fees, postal charges or physical delivery addresses required for delivery of the service.

2. Digital Delivery

After successful registration and payment confirmation, digital access or credits are allocated to the registered account. The app is activated only for the learner phone number registered by the account owner.

3. Delivery Time

Digital access is normally available shortly after successful payment confirmation. During beta testing, activation may require manual review or support if payment confirmation, phone activation or account verification fails.

4. Failed Delivery or Activation

If payment succeeds but credits or app access do not appear, contact lesegomohaule@gmail.com with the account email, payment reference and registered learner phone number.

5. Geographic Availability

SharperX is currently being tested for South African learners and South African CAPS-aligned school support.
